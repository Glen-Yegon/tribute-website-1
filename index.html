Celebration of a Life Well Lived

Born on 29th June 1934 and peacefully called to rest on 26th December 2025, Emilio Kanyari Ngotho lived a full and remarkable life marked by faith, integrity, and unwavering devotion to family and community.

A loving husband, father, grandfather, and great-grandfather, he was a pillar of unity whose home was always open, warm, and welcoming. His life exemplified generosity, discipline, humility, and a deep commitment to nurturing family bonds.

Educated in Kenya and abroad, Emilio served with distinction in public and private service for over 25 years, holding senior roles across key ministries and institutions. His career reflected diligence, integrity, and a lifelong dedication to national development.

Rooted in the Catholic faith, he lived by prayer, service, and compassion. He instilled values of honesty, hard work, and resilience, leaving behind a legacy that continues to guide generations.

His life reminds us that a life well lived is one grounded in love, faith, and service to others. Though deeply missed, his spirit lives on in the hearts of all who knew and loved him.
